Our segments within land services

Logistic and warehousing
Stuffing and stripping (loading/unloading of goods in containers) are the basis for the activities within the segment of logistics and warehousing.
A wide variety of goods are handled and stored, such as paper from SCA, tomato paste from Orkla Foods Sweden, steel rolls from Areco, and metals from Boliden.
Property and plots
The port manages rental of warehouses, plots, quays, and office spaces. In total, we manage approximately 965,000 square meters of land, including the North Harbour and Råå Harbour.

Keep track of the units


We always offer you the possibility to keep track of your units – information on each stage of the containers journey and whether the time frames are kept. With the PORTit system, we and our customers can follow freight traffic in real-time. Through wireless communication between trucks, hand-held terminals, tallymen, and the port’s central IT system, the container information is transmitted at the same moment as a physical transfer takes place. The information is published simultaneously in the portal for tracing units, where you can follow and trace all containers.
